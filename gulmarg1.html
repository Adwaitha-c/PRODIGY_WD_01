Gulmarg hill station is blessed with lofty snow-clad mountains, lush greenery, crystal-clear lakes and colourful flowers. It is a favourite holiday destination for travellers from all across the world. Also, it is one of the best skiing destinations in the world which is ideal for trained as well as amateur skiers. The several sightseeing options and the beauty of the places to visit in Gulmarg will take your breath away. The Gondola ride, Gulmarg gives you a birds eye view of the valley in the Pir Panjal range. 18-hole Gulmarg Golf Course is a must-visit among places to visit in Gulmarg as it is one of the highest golf courses in India.
